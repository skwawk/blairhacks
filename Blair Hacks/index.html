Welcome to Take Initiative!

In Maryland, citizens can influence their government through referendums and recalls. This website is designed to help citizens make referendums, learn about the requirements, find potential signatures, co-ordinate their canvasing, and more. You can also learn about other initiatives and volunteer as a potential signature.
